Our Story

Roller Motor Company was founded in **1952** by engineer and visionary, Arthur Roller, with a singular focus: to build vehicles that excel in reliability and performance. What started as a small, specialized auto shop has grown into a global leader in automotive innovation.

Today, RMC continues to honor its legacy by pushing the boundaries of technology, especially in sustainable and electric vehicle development. We believe driving should be an experience, not just a commute.

Our Mission

To build the most reliable, innovative, and performance-driven vehicles on the road, while prioritizing sustainable practices and uncompromising customer trust.

Our Vision

To lead the automotive world into an electric and autonomous future, setting new benchmarks for safety, luxury, and driving pleasure across all segments.
